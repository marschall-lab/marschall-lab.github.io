Post page format
================

The file name of your post has to be:

YYYY-MM-DD-short-title.md

The content has to be formatted like this:

---
title: This is the title of your post
authors:
- author1
- author2
---

What follows here is the content of your post with
(generic/github-style) [Markdown formatting](https://guides.github.com/features/mastering-markdown/).

To "disable" posts, add a prefix to the filenames, for instance a dot.
If no posts are found, the news section will be disabled.
